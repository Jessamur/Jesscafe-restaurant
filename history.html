Our
history

It all started with a cupcake and a dream.

Literally.

Jesscafé owner Jessica Marshall dozed off one night while eating a cupcake on her couch, and traversed a candy-coated, sugar-infused dreamscape that married her favorite things about bars, restaurants and bakeries. For hours, she reclined on a puffy cloud of candy floss, enjoying her cupcake with a fresh, farm-to-table meal and a signature cocktail.

Sure, it was just a dream, but it planted the seed of what was to become Jesscafé.

Today, that dream is a reality. Jesscafé welcomes visitors near and far. And we don't judge if you stay so long you fall asleep eating a cupcake.
